# KPI Definitions and Reporting Logic

## Project

RetailOps Insight — Retail Operations Analytics, Data Quality, and Support Dashboard

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators used in the RetailOps Insight project.

The goal is to make reporting logic clear, consistent, and explainable for SQL analysis, dashboards, and business review.

## Why KPI Definitions Matter

KPI definitions prevent confusion between similar metrics.

For example, raw revenue, completed sales, cancelled amount, and net sales are not the same thing.

Clear KPI definitions help ensure that reports, dashboards, and business decisions are based on consistent logic.
